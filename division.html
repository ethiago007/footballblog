Evolution of football

Football as we know it has evolved throughout generations. We are now in the modern state of football where various technology has been introduced into the game , making it sweeter to watch. We have the VAR which means Virutal assistant referee. We have the higher quality cameras, drones and so much more. As we all know, football spread all through the globe. We have the English premier league, Laliga, Serie A, Bundesliga, DFB Pokal league, AFC championship, Nigerian Football league, Super Diski and lots more. There are also major competition like UEFA champions league, Europa league, World cup, World cup club, AFCON, UEFA nations cup, UEFA euros cup and so many more. We have many football clubs just to mention few. Clubs like Manchester City,Manchester United, Barcelona, Real Madrid, Juventus, PSG, Bayern Munich, and so much more.
